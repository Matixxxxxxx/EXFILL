# Equipment Balance

Version: 0.1


# 1. Purpose

Equipment balance prevents one perfect setup.


# 2. Balance Factors


Every item is balanced through:


## Protection

How much advantage it gives.


## Cost

How much risk it creates.


## Weight

How it affects movement.


## Availability

How easy it is to obtain.


# 3. Golden Rule

The best equipment should be expensive, rare or difficult to use.


# 4. Goal

Players should create their own strategies.
