# Armor System

Version: 0.1


# 1. Purpose

Armor provides protection while creating economic and tactical choices.


# 2. Armor Protection Areas


Armor can protect:


## Chest

Main protection area.

Affects survival during firefights.


## Stomach

Protects important body area.


## Arms

Provides partial protection.


## Legs

Usually limited protection.


# 3. Armor Classes


Armor levels represent protection capability.


Low Tier:

Affordable equipment.

Used by beginners and budget players.


Medium Tier:

Balanced protection and cost.


High Tier:

Expensive equipment for dangerous raids.


# 4. Armor Tradeoffs


Better armor:

+
survival chance

-
higher cost

-
weight


# 5. Goal

Armor should increase options.

It should not make players invincible.
