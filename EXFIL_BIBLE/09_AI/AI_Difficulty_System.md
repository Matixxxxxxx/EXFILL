# 1. Purpose

# 2. Difficulty Factors


AI difficulty changes through:


Awareness:

How quickly AI detects players.


Equipment:

Weapons and protection.


Tactics:

Decision quality.


Numbers:

Amount of enemies.


# 3. Avoid

Do not only increase:

- health;
- damage;
- accuracy.
