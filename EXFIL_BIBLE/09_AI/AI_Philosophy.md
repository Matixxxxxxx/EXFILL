# PROJECT EXFIL

# AI Philosophy

Version: 0.1


# 1. Purpose

AI in EXFIL exists to create:

- pressure;
- atmosphere;
- unpredictable situations;
- additional gameplay.


AI should not replace PvP.

AI should make the world feel alive.


# 2. Core Philosophy

AI enemies should behave like characters with goals.

Not like shooting targets.


# 3. AI Goals


AI should:

- react to player actions;
- protect important locations;
- communicate;
- adapt to situations.


# 4. AI Should Create Stories


Example:

A player enters a warehouse.

AI detects movement.

Enemies call backup.

The fight creates noise.

Another squad arrives.


The AI changed the situation.


# 5. Avoid


Do not create:

- unfair AI;
- perfect aim;
- random impossible behavior.


# 6. Final Goal

Players should respect AI threats.
