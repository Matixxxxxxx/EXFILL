# PROJECT EXFIL

# AI Behavior System

Version: 0.1


# 1. Purpose

Controls how AI reacts to situations.


# 2. Behavior States


## Idle

Normal activity.


Examples:

- patrol;
- guard;
- work.


---

## Alert

AI suspects danger.


Actions:

- investigate;
- search area.


---

## Combat

AI engages enemies.


Actions:

- attack;
- use cover;
- communicate.


---

## Retreat

AI tries to survive.


Actions:

- escape;
- call help.


# 3. Goal

AI should transition between states naturally.
