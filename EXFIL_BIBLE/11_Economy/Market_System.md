# PROJECT EXFIL

# Market System

Version: 0.1


# 1. Purpose

The market allows players to trade resources.


# 2. Market Functions


Players can:

- buy;
- sell;
- compare prices;
- predict demand.


# 3. Market Philosophy


Prices should reflect:

- supply;
- demand;
- rarity;
- events.


# 4. Dynamic Market


Example:


Players extract many batteries.


Supply increases.


Price decreases.


Another resource becomes valuable.


# 5. Goal

Players should participate in the economy.
