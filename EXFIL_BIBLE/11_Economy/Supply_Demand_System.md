# Supply And Demand System

Version: 0.1


# 1. Purpose

Supply and demand creates economic changes.


# 2. Factors


Prices change because of:


Player actions.


Events.


Faction activity.


World changes.


# 3. Example


A city power station is destroyed.


Generators become rare.


Generator parts become expensive.


Players start searching for them.


# 4. Goal

The economy reacts to the world.
