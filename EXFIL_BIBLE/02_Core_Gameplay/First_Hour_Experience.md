# PROJECT EXFIL

# First Hour Experience

Version: 0.1


# 1. Purpose

The first hour determines whether a new player understands and enjoys EXFIL.

The goal is not to teach every system.

The goal is to create the first emotional connection.


# 2. First Launch

The player should immediately understand:


"You enter dangerous locations, collect valuable resources and try to escape alive."


Avoid:

- long tutorials;
- unnecessary explanations;
- overwhelming information.


# 3. First 10 Minutes


The player learns:

- movement;
- shooting;
- inventory;
- equipment;
- extraction concept.


The player should already feel:

"This is different from normal Roblox shooters."


# 4. First Raid


The first raid should include:


## Small Risk

The player should not lose everything immediately.


## Clear Goal

Example:

- find supplies;
- reach extraction;
- survive.


## First Tension Moment

The player should experience:

- hearing enemies;
- seeing danger;
- making a choice.


# 5. First Successful Extraction


This moment is extremely important.


The player should feel:

"I survived."


Not:

"I completed a tutorial."


# 6. First Death


Death should teach:

- why it happened;
- what could be improved.


The player should immediately want another attempt.


# 7. First Hour Goal


After one hour the player should understand:


- why raids are exciting;
- why equipment matters;
- why extraction matters;
- why they want to improve.
