# PROJECT EXFIL

# Market Position

Version: 0.1


# 1. Position

EXFIL aims to become a premium-quality extraction shooter experience inside Roblox.


# 2. Roblox Advantage

Roblox provides:

- large potential audience;
- social interaction;
- community growth;
- constant updates.


# 3. Competitive Goal

Do not compete by being bigger.

Compete by being more memorable.


# 4. Brand Identity

EXFIL should represent:

- tension;
- teamwork;
- survival;
- achievement.


# 5. Long-Term Goal

Build a game that players recognize as:

"The extraction shooter experience on Roblox."
