# PROJECT EXFIL

# Player Experience

Version: 0.1


# 1. Desired Experience

EXFIL should create a feeling of:

- tension;
- uncertainty;
- excitement;
- achievement.


The player should constantly ask:


"What should I do next?"


# 2. Before The Raid

The player should feel:

## Preparation

"I need the right equipment."


## Risk

"Can I afford to lose this?"


## Expectation

"What will happen this time?"


# 3. During The Raid

The player should feel:

## Awareness

"I need information."


## Tension

"Someone could be nearby."


## Decision Pressure

"Should I continue or leave?"


# 4. After The Raid

Successful extraction should create:

- satisfaction;
- confidence;
- motivation.


Death should create:

- understanding;
- desire to improve;
- motivation for another attempt.


# 5. Perfect Raid Example

A perfect raid is not:

The player finds the most expensive loot.


A perfect raid is:

The player enters with a plan.

Something unexpected happens.

The player adapts.

The team makes decisions.

The player survives.

The story is remembered.
