# PROJECT EXFIL

# Quest Types

Version: 0.1


# 1. Purpose

Different quest types create different gameplay.


# 2. Quest Categories


## Retrieval Quest

Purpose:

Find and extract specific items.


Examples:

- documents;
- technology;
- supplies.


---

## Elimination Quest

Purpose:

Remove threats.


Examples:

- bosses;
- hostile squads;
- dangerous NPCs.


---

## Exploration Quest

Purpose:

Discover locations.


Examples:

- hidden areas;
- abandoned facilities.


---

## Survival Quest

Purpose:

Complete difficult conditions.


Examples:

- survive raid;
- extract without certain equipment.


---

## Investigation Quest

Purpose:

Discover information.


Examples:

- investigate events;
- follow clues.


# 3. Goal

Different quests should support different playstyles.
