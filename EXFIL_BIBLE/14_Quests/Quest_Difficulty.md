# 1. Purpose

# 2. Difficulty Factors


Consider:

- required preparation;
- time;
- enemy strength.


# 3. Quest Tiers


Easy:

Learning.


Medium:

Normal progression.


Hard:

Experienced players.


Extreme:

High risk rewards.
