# PROJECT EXFIL

# Combat Philosophy

Version: 0.1


# 1. Purpose

Combat is the core interaction system of EXFIL.

Every firefight should create:

- tension;
- decision making;
- risk;
- memorable moments.


# 2. Combat Identity

EXFIL combat should feel:

- tactical;
- dangerous;
- skill-based;
- rewarding.


The goal is not:

"The player who shoots first always wins."


The goal is:

"The player who makes better decisions has a higher chance to win."


# 3. Combat Principles


## Information Matters

Knowing:

- enemy location;
- enemy equipment;
- environment;

should provide an advantage.


## Position Matters

Good positioning should beat careless aggression.


## Preparation Matters

Equipment affects possibilities.

However:

Equipment should not guarantee victory.


# 4. Combat Emotion

A good fight should create:

Before:

"Should I engage?"


During:

"Can I survive?"


After:

"Was that worth it?"


# 5. Combat Goal

Every firefight should have a story.
