# PROJECT EXFIL

# Death Experience

Version: 0.1


# 1. Purpose

Death is an important part of extraction gameplay.

However, death must feel meaningful.


# 2. After Death

The player should understand:

- what happened;
- who defeated them;
- what mistake was made.


# 3. Death Feedback


Possible information:

- damage received;
- enemy location;
- equipment lost.


# 4. Emotional Goal


The player should think:


"I can do better next time."


Not:


"There was nothing I could do."


# 5. Balance

Death creates tension.

Too much punishment creates frustration.
