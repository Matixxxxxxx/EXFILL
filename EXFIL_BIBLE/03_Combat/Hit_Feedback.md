# PROJECT EXFIL

# Hit Feedback

Version: 0.1


# 1. Purpose

Players need clear information during combat.


# 2. Feedback Types


Visual:

- damage effects;
- impact effects;
- animations.


Audio:

- weapon sounds;
- armor impact;
- hit confirmation.


Gameplay:

- health changes;
- injuries;
- status effects.


# 3. Design Goal

The player should understand:

"I hit the enemy."

"I was hit."

"What happened?"


# 4. Avoid

Avoid unclear combat where players cannot understand outcomes.
