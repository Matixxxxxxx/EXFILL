# Weapon Balance

Version: 0.1


# 1. Balance Philosophy

No weapon should be objectively the best.


Every weapon needs:

- advantages;
- disadvantages.


# 2. Balance Factors


Weapons are balanced through:


## Cost

Expensive weapons should create risk.


## Skill Requirement

Powerful weapons may require experience.


## Availability

Rare weapons should not become mandatory.


## Situation

Different weapons should dominate different situations.


# 3. Example


High-end rifle:

Advantages:

- accuracy;
- damage;
- customization.


Disadvantages:

- expensive;
- valuable loss;
- higher pressure.


# 4. Goal

Players should choose weapons because they match their strategy.

Not because one weapon is always correct.
