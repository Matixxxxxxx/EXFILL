# PROJECT EXFIL

# Weapon Statistics Model

Version: 0.1


# 1. Purpose

Weapons are balanced using multiple factors.

No single statistic determines power.


# 2. Main Statistics


## Damage

Amount of damage dealt.


## Fire Rate

Speed of shooting.


## Accuracy

Weapon precision.


## Recoil

Difficulty controlling weapon.


## Handling

Speed of:

- aiming;
- switching;
- movement.


## Range

Effective distance.


## Reliability

Chance of malfunction or condition problems.


## Weight

Impact on mobility.


# 3. Balance Formula


Weapon Value =


Damage

+

Control

+

Utility

-

Cost

-

Limitations


# 4. Important Rule

Higher statistics do not always mean better weapon.

A weapon must fit a situation.
