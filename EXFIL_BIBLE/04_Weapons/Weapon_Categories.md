# Weapon Categories

Version: 0.1


# 1. Purpose

Weapon categories create different combat styles.


# 2. Main Categories


## Assault Rifles

Role:

Universal combat.


Strengths:

- versatility;
- medium range effectiveness;
- customization.


Weaknesses:

- expensive ammunition;
- average specialization.


---

## Submachine Guns

Role:

Close range combat.


Strengths:

- mobility;
- high fire rate.


Weaknesses:

- poor long range.


---

## Shotguns

Role:

Close range destruction.


Strengths:

- extremely powerful nearby.


Weaknesses:

- limited range.


---

## Sniper Rifles

Role:

Long range precision.


Strengths:

- extreme distance effectiveness.


Weaknesses:

- slow;
- difficult positioning.


---

## Designated Marksman Rifles

Role:

Medium-long range precision.


Strengths:

- accuracy;
- flexibility.


Weaknesses:

- weaker close combat.


---

## Light Machine Guns

Role:

Suppression.


Strengths:

- large magazines;
- sustained fire.


Weaknesses:

- heavy;
- slower movement.


---

## Pistols

Role:

Backup weapon.


Strengths:

- always available;
- low risk.


Weaknesses:

- limited power.


---

## Melee Weapons

Role:

Silent emergency option.


Strengths:

- no ammunition;
- stealth.


Weaknesses:

- dangerous.


# 3. Rule

Every category must have a reason to exist.
