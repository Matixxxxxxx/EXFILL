# PROJECT EXFIL

# Weapon Rarity System

Version: 0.1


# 1. Purpose

Rarity creates value and excitement.

However, rarity should not equal automatic power.


# 2. Weapon Tiers


## Common

Standard equipment.

Easy to obtain.

Used by beginners and budget players.


---

## Uncommon

Improved versions.

Better characteristics.


---

## Rare

Limited availability.

Special modifications.


---

## Legendary

Extremely valuable items.

Unique properties or history.


# 3. Important Rule

A common weapon in skilled hands can defeat rare equipment.


# 4. Rarity Sources


Rarity can come from:

- condition;
- modifications;
- history;
- limited availability;
- special versions.
