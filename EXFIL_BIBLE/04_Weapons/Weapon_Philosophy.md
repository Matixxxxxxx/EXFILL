# PROJECT EXFIL

# Weapon Philosophy

Version: 0.1


# 1. Purpose

Weapons are one of the most important emotional systems in EXFIL.

A weapon represents:

- player choice;
- risk;
- identity;
- experience.


# 2. Weapon Design Goal

Every weapon should feel unique.

Players should have reasons to choose different weapons.


# 3. Weapon Is More Than Statistics

A weapon is not only:

- damage;
- fire rate;
- accuracy.


A weapon includes:

- sound;
- appearance;
- customization;
- history;
- availability;
- cost.


# 4. Weapon Identity

Every weapon needs:

## Strength

Why players choose it.


## Weakness

What limits it.


## Situation

Where it performs best.


Example:


AK Platform:

Strength:

- reliability;
- availability;
- customization.


Weakness:

- recoil;
- lower precision.


Situation:

medium range combat.


# 5. Emotional Value

Rare and personalized weapons should create attachment.

Players should remember their equipment.


# 6. Design Rule

No weapon should exist only to increase the number of items.
