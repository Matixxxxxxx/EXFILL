# Signature Weapons

Version: 0.1


# 1. Purpose

Some weapons should become legendary among players.


# 2. What Makes A Signature Weapon


A weapon can become special because of:

- rarity;
- unique appearance;
- special modification;
- player history.


# 3. Examples


A weapon used to defeat a boss.


A weapon extracted from an extremely dangerous location.


A weapon owned by a famous player.


# 4. Important Rule

The strongest weapon is not always the most memorable.

The story behind it creates value.
